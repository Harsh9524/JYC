Technical, Movies/Photography Club

Composition of Movie, Photography and Createch the Technical Club of Jaypee University of Information Technology provides personal growth,leadership and learning opportunities in technology, innovation, photography, design and engineering. It achieves this by conducting various interactive workshops and by hands on fun teaching sessions on topics like webdevelopment, cloudcomputing, networking, cyber security and robotics. The technical Event of the college 'Murious' is organized by createch. The movie club takes the responsibility for telecasting cricket matches and movies which is a great recreational activity for the students. During the annual fest 'Le Fiestus'the Movie Club organizes many fun events. We keep our memories alive in the photographs, the budding photographers of the photography club capture all the events and have mastery in skills like photoshop, videoediting, graphic designing and many more.
